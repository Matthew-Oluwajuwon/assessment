Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish that features al dente spaghetti noodles generously coated in a rich, savory Bolognese sauce. This hearty sauce is traditionally made with ground meat, such as beef or pork, slowly simmered with diced onions, garlic, carrots, and celery in a flavorful tomato base. Infused with aromatic herbs like basil, oregano, and thyme, the Bolognese sauce boasts a robust and satisfying flavor profile that perfectly complements the tender pasta. Topped with a sprinkle of grated Parmesan cheese, Spaghetti Bolognese offers a comforting and fulfilling dining experience that embodies the essence of Italian cuisine.
